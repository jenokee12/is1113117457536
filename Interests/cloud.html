iCloud

iCloud is a cloud storage and cloud computing service from Apple Inc. The service provides its users with means to store data such as documents,photos, and music on remote servers for download to iOS, macOS or Windows devices, to share and send data to other users, and to manage their Apple devices if lost or stolen.

Netflix remembers exactly where you last stopped watching something and picks it right back up from there hours, days or even weeks later. It seems like magic.
